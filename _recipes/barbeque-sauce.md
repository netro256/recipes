ingredients:
- 2 tbsp red or white wine
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 cup ketchup
- 1 cup water
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp Tabasco sauce
- 3/4 tsp black pepper
- 2 Medium Onions (finely chopped)
- dash of garlic powder

directions:
- Mix all ingredients together in a pot and cook for 15-20 minutes.
