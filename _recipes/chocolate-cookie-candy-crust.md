ingredients:
- 11 Oreos, crushed
- 1/4 cup M&M's, copped
- 3 tbsp butter

directions:
- In a small bowl, stir together crushed cookies and chopped candy.
- Stir in melted butter until combined.
- Press mixture evenly onto the bottom of a greased spring-formed pan (pie pan).
