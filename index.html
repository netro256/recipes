Measurement Conversions

1 Tbsp = 15 mL or 3 tsp

1 tsp = 5 mL

1 fl oz = 6 tsp or 2 Tbsp or 30 mL

1 Cup = 48 tsp or 16 tbsp or 8 fl oz

1 Pint = 2 Cups or 16 fl oz

1 Quart = 4 Cups or 2 Pints or 32 fl oz

1 Gallon = 4 Quarts or 128 fl oz
